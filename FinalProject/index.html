Welcome to our website! Over the course of five weeks, students of SMASH have been developing skills in mathematics, biology and computer science that correlate to infectious diseases. Our final project focouses on the Influenza, or more commonly known as the flu. For our final we created a prototype, analyzed graphs and data, created a website, and collected informatoin on the influenza in California.
