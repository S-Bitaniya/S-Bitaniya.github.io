Combating The Influenza In California

Welcome to our website! Over the course of five weeks, students of SMASH have been developing skills in mathematics, biology and computer science that correlate to infectious diseases. Our final project focuses on the Influenza, or more commonly known as the flu. For our final we created a prototype, analyzed graphs and data, created a website, and collected information on the influenza in California.

We hope you find your browsing enjoyable and accesible, please leave us feedback so that we can better our website to further improve your experience.
